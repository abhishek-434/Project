Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Booking a tour is easy! You can browse our available packages online, select your preferred tour, choose your travel dates, and complete the booking process directly on our website. If you prefer personal assistance, you can contact our booking team by phone or email, and they'll guide you through the process.

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. For certain destinations, we also offer payment plans where you can pay a deposit to secure your booking and the remaining balance closer to your departure date.

Our standard cancellation policy offers a full refund if canceled 60 days or more before departure, a 50% refund if canceled 30-59 days before departure, and no refund if canceled less than 30 days before departure. However, policies may vary depending on the specific tour and destination. We also offer travel insurance options to protect your booking against unforeseen circumstances.
