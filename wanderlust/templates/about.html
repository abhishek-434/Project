About Wanderlust

Our Story

Wanderlust was born from a passion for exploration and a desire to share the world's wonders with fellow travelers.

Founded in 2010 by a group of avid travelers with over 30 years of combined experience in the tourism industry, Wanderlust has grown from a small local agency to an international travel company serving thousands of happy customers every year.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to create authentic, immersive travel experiences that go beyond the typical tourist attractions. We wanted to help travelers connect with local cultures, discover hidden gems, and create memories that would last a lifetime.

Today, we remain true to that mission while continuously evolving to meet the changing needs of modern travelers. We're proud of our growth, but even prouder of the countless adventures we've helped create and the positive impact we've made in the communities we visit.
