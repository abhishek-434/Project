How It All Started

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate travelers, Wanderlust Adventures began as a dream to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone.

What started as weekend hiking trips with friends has grown into a global travel company that has helped over 10,000 adventurers explore the world's most incredible destinations.
